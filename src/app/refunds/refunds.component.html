Refunds and Returns Policy

At Home Goods Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please review our refunds and returns policy below.

Returns and Exchanges

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of the date of purchase. To be eligible for a return or exchange, the item must be in its original condition and packaging, with all tags and labels attached. We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet these conditions.

Refunds

If you are returning an item for a refund, we will credit the original payment method for the purchase price of the item, minus any shipping and handling fees. Refunds may take up to 10 business days to process, depending on your bank or credit card issuer.

Exchanges

If you are exchanging an item, we will ship the replacement item to you at no additional charge, as long as it is of equal or lesser value than the original item. If the replacement item is of greater value, we will charge you for the difference.

Shipping and Handling

You are responsible for shipping and handling fees for returns and exchanges, unless the item is defective or damaged. In this case, we will cover the shipping and handling fees for the return or exchange.

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us within 7 days of receiving the item. We will provide instructions on how to return the item, and will cover all shipping and handling fees for the return or exchange.

Final Sale Items

Final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged, except in the case of a defective or damaged item.

How to Return or Exchange an Item

To return or exchange an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at [Insert Contact Information] to request a return or exchange.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, with all tags and labels attached.
  3. Include a copy of the original receipt or packing slip with the item.
  4. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer service team.

If you have any questions or concerns about our refunds and returns policy, please contact us at info@homegoods.co.za.
