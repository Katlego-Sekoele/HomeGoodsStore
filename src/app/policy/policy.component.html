Privacy Policy

Home Goods Store ("we" or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it.

  1. Information We Collect We may collect the following types of personal information from you:
    • Contact information, such as your name, email address, and phone number
    • Billing and payment information, such as your billing address and credit card number
    • Demographic information, such as your age, gender, and location
    • Information about your preferences and interests, such as your favorite home goods, furniture, or kitchenware products
    • Information about your visits to our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information

  2. How We Use Your Information We may use your personal information for the following purposes:
    • To process and fulfill your orders for home goods, furniture, or kitchenware products
    • To communicate with you about your orders or customer service inquiries
    • To personalize your shopping experience by showing you products that match your interests
    • To send you marketing communications, such as emails or newsletters, about our products and promotions
    • To improve our website and services, such as by analyzing customer behavior and preferences

  3. Information Sharing and Disclosure We may share your personal information with third parties in the following circumstances:
    • With service providers who help us with payment processing, shipping, or marketing
    • With other companies owned by or affiliated with us
    • With government or law enforcement authorities if required by law or to protect our legal rights

  4. Your Choices and Rights You have the following choices and rights regarding your personal information:
    • You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.
    • You can access and update your personal information by logging into your account on our website.
    • You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain limitations.

  5. Data Security We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a revised version on our website. Your continued use of our website after any changes to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such changes.

    6. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@homegoods.co.za.
